What we actually do differently

👥

25–30 students per batch, not 60

Every student gets called on, every doubt gets addressed. We don't run lecture halls.

📝

Weekly tests with parent reports

Every week, students are tested. Every month, parents receive a performance summary with specific areas to improve.

📅

Planned syllabus coverage

No rushing before exams. The full syllabus is covered by January with 2 months left for revision and practice.

💬

Saturday doubt-solving sessions

Free weekly sessions where students bring any question from the week. No new topics — just clearing confusion.

📊

Board + competitive dual track

11th–12th students prepare for boards and JEE/NEET simultaneously. One admission, both goals covered.

📱

Parent WhatsApp group updates

Attendance notifications, test scores, and schedule changes — parents stay informed without having to ask.
